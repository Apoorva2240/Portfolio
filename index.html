My story

My marketing journey didn't start in a boardroom; it started in the heart of luxury hospitality and the fast-paced world of culinary arts. For over two years, I was on the front lines of creating premium brand experiences, learning firsthand what drives customer loyalty and satisfaction at world-renowned brands like The Leela Palace and Taj. I discovered a passion for understanding the "why" behind a consumer's choice and a drive to create memorable moments.

To add the science to the art of customer engagement, I'm now pursuing a Master's in Digital and Data Marketing. Today, I swap chef's whites for data dashboards, using tools like Google Analytics, Power Bi, and Semrush to craft data-driven campaign strategies. My experience ranges from developing the go-to-market plan for a food-tech startup to executing A/B tests for social media campaigns in simulations. I thrive on connecting the dots between real-world human insight and digital performance.

In my free time, you can find me tuning in to catch the next football game or hiking up a trail or diving into the next videogame the internet is going crazy about. I'm excited to bring my creative energy and experience to your team. Let's build something amazing together!
