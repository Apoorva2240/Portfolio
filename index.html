A data-driven marketing professional with a unique foundation in luxury hospitality and the culinary arts. With over two years of experience leading teams and managing operations in high-pressure, customer-centric environments like The Leela Palace, I have a deep, first-hand understanding of what drives consumer loyalty and creates a premium brand experience.
