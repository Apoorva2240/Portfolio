My story

I'm a Digital Marketing specialist currently completing an MSc in Digital & Data Marketing at NEOMA Business School, and interning as an SEO & Analytics specialist at Lallemand Brewing in Toulouse, France — working across SEO strategy, GA4/GTM implementation, and multilingual content optimization.

My approach is grounded in data. I work at the intersection of search visibility and measurement — mapping keywords against real traffic signals, building tracking architectures that give teams an honest view of user behavior, and translating that into clear strategic recommendations. I've worked across B2B e-commerce, food-tech startups, and agency-style simulations, which means I'm comfortable with both technical implementation and campaign thinking.

What gives me a different perspective is where I started. Before digital marketing, I spent three years in luxury hospitality at brands like The Leela Palace and Taj — environments where the guest experience is everything and every touchpoint is intentional. That grounding in premium brand thinking still shapes how I approach consumer journeys today.

Outside of work, I'm usually watching football, finding a new trail, or deep in whatever game the internet won't stop talking about.
